Luca Brasi

Album Launch Show

Friday 12th FEBRUARY 2021 - 7.30pm

Tasmania’s own Luca Brasi are set to release their 5th studio album, titled Everything Is Tenuous on Friday 12th February.
To celebrate, the band are hosting a massive headline show at The Goods Yard in Hobart on the release night.

This will undoubtedly be a very special show from Luca Brasi, especially given the current state of live music in rest of the world right now. If you’re in Tassie, or you can get to Tassie, don’t miss this chance to see Luca Brasi play their first full band headline show in well over a year. Playing all the faves from their back catalogue, plus showcasing tracks from the new album.
Those in attendance can pre-purchase a copy of the album on CD or Vinyl, with a FREE limited edition signed poster. Check your email receipt when you buy a ticket for a link to these exclusive bundles.
IN ADDITION – The first 30 sales of the Album/Poster will get to attend a meet & greet with the band at sound check.
Tickets are strictly limited due to COVID restrictions, so don’t delay and grab yourself a ticket now

Strictly 18+ event
