The Garden Gathering

[CANCELLED] BoilUp

Sunday 27th December 2020 11.30am

On Sunday Afternoon, the whole family can enjoy the harmonies of BoilUp. The roots of the 10-piece band stretch from the traditional owners of Tasmania, New Zealand and Fiji, to as far away as Ireland, England and Croatia; and mix traditional reggae with funk and R&B.

 

[RESCHEDULED] The Wolfe Brothers

Monday 28th December 2020 11.30pm

In the evening Tasmania and Australia’s most popular country rock band The Wolfe Brothers will perform a jaw-dropping live show. The Wolfe Brothers have music in their DNA and have never been the band to stay in one lane. They fuse together pub, new and old country, with their heartfelt songs.

The Blues Brothers Revival Band

Monday 28th December 2020 11.30am

Monday is for all things yesteryear, as we make a trip down memory lane with the Blues Brothers Revival band. The band will spoil you with a 2-hour non-stop high energy show, full of songs, dance routines and lines from the movie. Let it be Beatles

Monday 28th December 2020 5.30pm

This year marks the 50th anniversary of the classic album ‘Let It Be’, the twelfth and final studio album by The Beatles. Let It Be Beatles, a band consisting of an all-star lineup with Tom Wolfe, Pete Cornelius, Scott Targett, Jethro Pickett, Beau Thomas, Tim Rozemulder, Joe Weller and Louisa Hogue, will deliver the album in entirety from start to finish.
