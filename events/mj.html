Maddy Jane - The Tour of Tassie

With Special Guests

Friday 5th MARCH 2021 - 7.00pm

It’s been two long years but finally Maddy Jane is back in Tassie playing headline shows – and she’s bringing her full band with her.

Touring her triple j feature album ‘Not All Bad Or Good’ – this is Hobart's chance to hear the new songs live for the first time at The Goods Shed.
Maddy’s last tour sold out, so jump on tickets now before they’re gone. Tickets are strictly limited, and COVID-safe rules will apply.

Supports:
The Tinderboxers
The Brocken Girls Club
Seedy Minx
