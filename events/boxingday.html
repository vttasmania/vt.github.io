Boxing Day Party

A. Swayze and the Ghosts & more

SATURDAY 26th DEC 2020 - 12:30pm

Drain your eggnog and make sure your stocking’s empty by the time this year’s boxing day party kicks off. The traditional Boxing Day event is back with all your favorite artists. This year is held in the beautiful Botanical Gardens. Tickets are limited, get them quick.

Lineup: A. Swayze and the Ghosts
Slag Queens
Chase City
Kudu Joy
The Tinderboxers
Lazer Baby
Lennon Wells
Zac Henderson & Katie Wilson

This event is strictly 18+.

The event will be produced in accordance with the Tasmanian Health Guidelines. All ticket holders will receive an email with the latest updates prior to the event.

Please be aware of fake events on social media, and be advised that this performance is not streamed. The official ticketing agency for this show is Oztix. Tickets purchased through third parties such as Viagogo, Gumtree, Facebook are done at your own risk. Fraudulent tickets will not be honored.
