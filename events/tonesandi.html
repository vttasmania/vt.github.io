Tones and I

New Date & Venue Upgrade


SATURDAY 1st MAY - 6.00pm



IMPORTANT - PLEASE READ


We understand there is a lot of information here but we appreciate you taking the time to read this carefully.


1. THIS IS A SEATED EVENT - BYO CHAIR
This is a SEATED event so please BYO high back chair. Due to the regulations imposed by Public Health: no chair - no entry.

There are only three reasons to leave your seat:
● To purchase drinks, food or merch
● Use the toilets or smoking zone
● Seek assistance from First Aid

Bean bags, picnic blankets & low line chairs are not permitted.


2. PLEASE ARRIVE WITH YOUR SOCIAL GROUP
Please arrive with your social group to ensure you are assigned into the same zone. If you arrive separately from your group of friends we can not guarantee you can sit together. You have to stay in this zone for the duration of the event. If you leave your zone, you will be ejected and banned from future events.

3. COVIDSafe Practices

You know the drill:
● Maintain 1.5m distance from others where practicable
● Wash & sanitise your hands regularly
● Sneeze and cough in your elbow
● Stay home when you feel unwell or show any symptoms of COVID-19
● Do not attend if you are currently required to self-isolate, or are a returned traveller from a medium or high risk hotspot


4. TRANSPORT / PARKING
 ● If you are walking from the city we advise you to take both walking bridges, from Liverpool Street to the Domain, then over the Remembrance Bridge.
● There is a drop off / pick up point at the Aquatic Centre for taxis and Ubers.
● McVilly Drive is closed off, there is no drop off for Ubers and taxis. They will be refused, increasing your fair.
● Please only use these access points. Failure to adhere to traffic rules may lead to refusal of entry to the event.
● If you do decide to drive you can park at the Domain. From here you can use the Remembrance Bridge to reach the Regatta Grounds.


5. LEAVING THE VENUE AFTER THE SHOW
 Please be prepared and allow some time to leave the venue if you are driving or need to catch a taxi. Everyone will be leaving at the same time so expect delays. If you decide to walk back to town, please beware of traffic and stay on sidewalks.

6. WHAT TO BRING
 ● A high back folding chair - this is required for entry
● Warm clothes and a cozy blanket
● Your tickets, we prefer them digital
● Credit card or debit card - Contactless payments are preferred
● A phone with the Check-In-TAS app installed
● A valid government issued ID
● Sealed water bottles or empty reusable water bottles (no glass)
● Food trucks are serving delicious meals all night, no need to bring your own.


7. WHAT NOT TO BRING
 ● BYO alcohol - Anyone found with BYO alcohol will be refused entry without a refund
● Professional film equipment
● Umbrellas, shade tents
● Glassware and metal cutlery
● Weapons, fireworks, illicit substances, pets (other than assistance dogs)
● Aerosol cans
● All your worries – definitely leave those at home!


8. REASONS FOR REFUSAL / REMOVAL WITHOUT REFUND
● Leaving your assigned zone
● Possession of contraband or BYO alcohol
● Intoxication
● Anti-social behaviour
● You have been banned from Vandemonian Touring events


9. OTHER IMPORTANT INFO
● Merch: There will be merch for sale
● Bags: Security staff will check all bags upon entry. Any person in possession of prohibited items will be refused entry
● Cloakroom: There are NO cloakroom facilities
● Food & Drinks: We have a wide range of food and drinks available. You can fill up your water bottle at the free water stations
● First Aid will be in attendance – please approach any of our staff if you need assistance
● Pass outs: There will be strictly no pass outs. Please ensure you have everything you need as you can not leave the event and come back.
● Smoking: Smoking is only allowed in the designated smoking areas, so please be mindful of others. We will not sell cigarettes at this event



We are pleased to announce that we will be working closely with the Department of Health and Human Resources, Worksafe and Tasmania Police to ensure a safe and fun event for everyone.
