HOBART UNI BAR

The Venue

One of Hobart’s iconic venues, the Hobart Uni Bar has been home to live music for over three decades. The watering hole for generations of UTAS students, the Hobart Uni Bar has existed as an accessible space for all music lovers.


From big name bands to emerging artists, the Hobart Uni Bar has been a cornerstone of events for the University of Tasmania's campus.

Live Music & Functions

The venue hosts a range of events, including live music shows, functions, cultural events, fundraisers and quizzes.

Artists that have graced the stage include Paul Kelly, Peking Duk, The Amity Affliction, Jungle, Pendulum, Ball Park Music, Bliss n Eso, Kisschasy and Spiderbait, among many others.
