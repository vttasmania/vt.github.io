Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question?

Here are frequently-asked questions about our events.

Are events going ahead during the COVID-19 pandemic?

Our events comply with all current Covid-19 restrictions in Tasmania. Due to the changing nature of restrictions, be prepared to abide any precautions necessary.

Are there "pass-outs" at my event?

No. Unfortunately there are strictly no pass outs at Vandemonian events.

Can I have the ticket on my phone?

Yes. We prefer this over paper tickets. Please make sure that the QR code is clearly visible.

What form of ID will I need?

You need a valid Government issued ID.

I want to sell my ticket, where can can I safely resell?

We only recommend reselling your ticket using Tixel.com
