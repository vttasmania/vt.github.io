Tones and I

May 2021

Global breakout artist Tones and I brought her live show to Hobart in May 2021, after a massive year that saw the artist climb to the top of the charts in 25+ countries. Tones And I's live act toured the international stage with multiple headline shows selling out around the world.

An incredible performance for an incredible show, Tones and I's journey from busker to chart-topping musician has seen her meteoric rise resonate throughout Australia's music scene.
